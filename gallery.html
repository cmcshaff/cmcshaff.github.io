Soap-film microscopy

Gallery

Thin films of soapy water, a few hundred nanometres thick, split and recombine light into bands of colour — the same interference that makes a bubble shimmer. I photograph these films through a microscope in the seconds before they drain and pop. Prints are available; say hello if a piece speaks to you.

More of the series lives on Instagram and in my print shop.
