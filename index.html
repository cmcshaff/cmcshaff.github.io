Theoretical & systems biology · Dynamical systems

Connor McShaffrey

I study the viability of living systems — when an organism survives, when it dies, and the geometry of the boundary between them. I build dynamical-systems models of agents under viability constraints and map the global structure of their survival outcomes.

Incoming Postdoctoral Fellow · Vivarium Lab, UConn Health · Fall 2026
