Dissertation

A Theoretical Approach to the Viability of Agential Systems

Develops viability space decomposition — an extension of dynamical systems theory whose new manifolds partition single- and multi-agent state spaces into regions of similar existential outcomes — and lays the foundation for a bifurcation theory around these structures.

Committee: Randall Beer (chair), James Glazier (minor advisor), Ann-Sophie Barwich, and Jacob Foster.
